Our Vision

Vision

How We're Helping

SAPPHIRE, a student organization under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University, is an amalgamation of smart working, fun loving, innovative students who work towards making sure that students have an ambience that has an aura of innovativeness. We incorporate within ourselves the mentality of thinking big, finding new and enjoyable ways of cracking academics, pushing our limits and finding new horizons to our personalities. We organize events which helps the students to see, examine and improve the mental, physical and social aspects of their life. If you are the one who wants to be not someone else but the best version of yourself, if you are seeking your horizons and want to break out your edges, we are here to stand, walk and run on that path to your better self. Our motto is to take everyone together and march ahead, exactly as our slogan says- ONE TEAM ONE DREAM.

Event Details

A confident, well-prepared, flexible and an effective presenter is the one who catches the attention of each and every audience. Being a good presenter is one of the most essential things in a professional life. Student organization "Sapphire" under the aegis of Division of Student Welfare, Lovely Professional University is organizing "VOICE OUT 2.0", a workshop to help you all out in bringing your best in public speaking and anchoring. "Skills are cheap, Passion is priceless" is the motto of our organization which inspires each and everyone of us. Dedication and passion is the key to success. And we are here to fuel up your skills which leads to your success. This workshop is not only about work but also includes fun. The presence of The Anchor of LPU, the one and only Vishnu is expected and the rest is assured.
All about the event : 1. Speaker for the Workshop is M.B. Vishnu. 2. Opportunity to enhance debating and speaking skills through practical activities. 3. Perfect opportunity to earn fame and enhance your personality.

Time: 5:00 PM - 7:30 PM
